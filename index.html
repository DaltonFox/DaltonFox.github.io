About Me

Hello, my name is Dalton, a game developer and software engineer from
St. Louis Missouri. I started programming at the age of ten building games and mods for Unreal Tournament with my father in Unreal Engine 2.

I have since grown my passion for gaming technologies into a career in software engineering and game development. I went to Shawnee State University for my Bachelor of Science in Digital Simulation and Gaming Engineering Technology.

I have over eight years of industry experience and enjoy diving into new technologies and solving new problems that I don't have experience with. I've worked with everything from compilers and embedded hardware to full-stack and cloud technologies. Valuing myself as both a technical leader and as a hands-on engineer.

Finding ways to combine the unique creative aspect of the gaming industry with the challenging real-world of software engineering is where my experience provides the most value. The way we view and interact with our work is just as important as the problems we aim to solve.
